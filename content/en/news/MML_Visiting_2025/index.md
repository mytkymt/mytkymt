I started a research visit at the [Morphing Matter Lab](https://morphingmatter.org/) at UC Berkeley from October 13, 2025 to December 11, 2025.
The Morphing Matter Lab, led by Professor [Lining Yao](https://morphingmatter.org/member/lining-yao), focuses on the discovery and study of morphing material mechanisms, as well as algorithms for their computational design and fabrication pipelines. The lab is interested in applications that contribute to planetary and human wellbeing.

During my visit, I will collaborate with lab members on projects related to FoodHCI. I look forward to learning from this research environment and contributing to ongoing projects.
